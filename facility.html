Our Facility

Private, fully equipped, and designed for serious training

A Professional Space Built for Serious Training

Rival Training's private facility at 1642 Bath Road was built from the ground up for coaches and athletes who mean business. No distractions, no overcrowding — just the equipment and space you need to do your best work.

What's Inside

Strength Floor

A fully stocked free weights area with barbells, dumbbells, plates, and a squat rack setup built for serious lifters. Everything you need to run progressive strength programming.

Cardio & Conditioning

Dedicated space and equipment for HIIT, circuit training, and aerobic work — from sleds and battle ropes to rowing and cycling. Built to support any conditioning protocol.

Functional Training Area

Open floor space for mobility work, bodyweight training, plyometrics, and kettlebell circuits. Clean rubber flooring with enough room to move freely.

Coaching Station

A dedicated area for program review, whiteboard sessions, and check-ins. Perfect for starting and finishing sessions with intent — not just walking in cold and guessing.

Private Booking

Sessions are booked privately — no shared floor time with strangers during your hour.

Locker Rooms

Clean, well-maintained change rooms for clients arriving before and after work.

Free Parking

Dedicated parking on-site — no meters, no circling the block, no excuses to be late.

Modern Amenities

Bluetooth audio, water station, and a comfortable environment that matches the quality of coaching.

Easy to Find

Located at 1642 Bath Road, Kingston — convenient access from across the city.

Clean & Maintained

Equipment is inspected, sanitized, and maintained regularly. Professional standards, always.

See It for Yourself

Book a session or a discovery call — come tour the space and meet the team before you commit to anything.
